Meet The Crew!




Sharen



Sharen has been a Colorist at Head Hunters for the past eight years and has ten years of color and styling experience. She joined the HH team in our mentorship program upon obtaining her license. After two years of working under our Master Colorist, Sharen became a full Colorist and now specializes in unique highlighting techniques, such as balayage and “babylights.”







Brittney


Hi I’m Brittney, and I’ve been a Colorist at Head Hunters for the past five years and has over 10 years of color and stylist experience. The wildest color I’ve ever dyed her hair was pastel blue and it was my favorite! I am always down for a fun new haircut and have a personal taste for fun colors!






Stacey


Stacey Rose, has been a Master Colorist at Head Hunters for the past five years and has over 15 years of color and styling experience. Stacey is trained and certified in multiple color systems and enjoys using her knowledge to create exactly what her clients are envisioning when they sit down in her chair.






Mackenzie


Mackenzie is a hairstylist known for her sleek, modern looks inspired by the city she lives in. Mackenzie’s work has appeared in City Mag and she has worked with Jefferys Photography on a Spring inspired shoot featuring Beyonce. Mackenzie has over 15 years of experience in hair styling.
